Bronze sculpture for warmth, patina, and touch

Bronze is strongest when its warmth, edge highlights, surface depth, and long-term patina are controlled for the site. WEIERYANG uses bronze as an architectural material route for gardens, resorts, water features, and public landscapes rather than a generic luxury finish.
