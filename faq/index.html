Custom Outdoor Sculpture FAQ for Landscape, Resort and Public Space Projects

Answers to common questions about custom outdoor sculpture, materials, site review, fabrication, export packing, overseas shipping and installation support.

FAQ

What types of custom sculptures do you make?

We create custom outdoor sculptures for gardens, resorts, hotels, water features, public landscapes, civic spaces and private estates. Projects can include stone sculpture, bronze sculpture, stainless steel sculpture, corten steel sculpture and mixed-material landscape pieces.

Who do you usually work with?

We work with landscape designers, resort developers, hotel project teams, private estate owners, public space planners, architects and overseas buyers who need custom sculpture design, fabrication and export support.

Can you support resort, hotel and public landscape projects?

Yes. We can review the site condition, viewing distance, landscape sequence, foundation logic, material route, packing method and installation requirements for resort, hotel and public landscape sculpture projects.

Which materials are suitable for outdoor sculpture?

Common outdoor sculpture materials include bronze, stone, granite, stainless steel and corten steel. The best choice depends on the site, weather exposure, water contact, maintenance expectation, visual style and installation condition.

Is 316L stainless steel suitable for coastal environments?

316L stainless steel is often preferred for coastal, humid or water-adjacent environments because it offers stronger corrosion resistance than standard stainless steel. The final material choice should still consider surface finish, maintenance access and site exposure.

Can you work from drawings, site photos or concept sketches?

Yes. A useful project brief can include landscape drawings, site photos, target size, viewing distance, preferred material, destination country, deadline and installation context. These details help us review scale, structure, packing and fabrication risk before quotation.

How do you review sculpture scale for a landscape site?

We review scale based on site drawings, pedestrian movement, viewing distance, surrounding architecture, landscape edges, water features, foundation position and installation clearance. A good sculpture should fit the site sequence, not only look good in isolation.

Do you provide structural or engineering coordination?

For large or permanent outdoor sculptures, we can support structural coordination, segmentation planning, base logic, trial assembly, packing method and installation guidance. Project-specific engineering requirements should be confirmed according to the final design and local site conditions.

How do you handle overseas packing and shipping?

Large sculptures are reviewed for segmentation, surface protection, crate design, loading method, export route and handling risk. Packing is planned according to material, finish, size, destination country and installation sequence.

Do you provide installation guidance for large sculptures?

Yes. We can provide installation reference, assembly sequence, foundation coordination suggestions and handling notes. For complex projects, installation requirements should be discussed early together with site drawings and local contractor conditions.

What information is needed before quotation?

Before quotation, please send site drawings, site photos, target size, preferred material, destination country, installation deadline, usage scenario and any concept sketch or reference image. These details help us estimate material, fabrication, packing and delivery requirements more accurately.

Can you provide material samples or finish references?

Yes. Depending on the project, we can discuss material samples, finish references, patina direction, brushed or polished surface, stone texture and other surface details before final production.
