Typical project route: resort arrival sculpture

A resort arrival sculpture should be reviewed through vehicle and pedestrian approach, hotel brand tone, night lighting, foundation position, guest photo angles and maintenance access. Common material routes include brushed stainless steel with bronze, stone base systems or mirror-finish stainless steel when reflection is part of the landscape.

Typical project route: water feature sculpture

Water feature sculpture requires early review of splash exposure, humidity, drainage, pump access, stone edges, slip risk, surface finish and installation sequence. 316L stainless steel, bronze and stone combinations are often considered for water-adjacent environments.

Typical project route: public landscape sculpture

Public landscape sculpture should be planned for viewing distance, touch points, safety, structure, anti-corrosion treatment, segmentation, trial assembly, shipping size and local installation coordination. The project should not be priced from an image alone.
