Material planning comes before decoration

Outdoor sculpture is shaped by weather exposure, water contact, viewing distance, touch points, drainage, maintenance and installation conditions. WEIERYANG helps buyers choose bronze, stone, 316L stainless steel, brushed stainless steel, corten steel and mixed-material routes according to the project site.
